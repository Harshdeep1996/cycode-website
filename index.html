What is Cycode?


Cycode is a platform where we welcome all the coding superheroes (“Every error free code we run makes us feel no less than a superhero” –Guilty hackers). We work on the fundamentals of bringing together the developer community across the country as well as our corporate sector. Our aim is to endow knowledge, inspiration, innovation, and job opportunities through the hackathon culture. To say, we are an organization, but absolutely incomplete without our hackers and supporters!

  • We give primordial value to learning.

  • We aim to blend the diverse work fields.

  • We care about social impact, for the good.

  • We love helping our hackers.

  • We believe in free hackathons for all.

  • We are awesome, but learning from our doings.

Frequently Asked Questions

For Organizers :

How can Cycode help hackathon organizers?

As Cycode and hackathon organizers will organize events together, so we would help you with getting sponsors, developing your hackathon website and even with the organizing of the hackathon. Plus we help you make friends at the right places.

Do we charge anything for powering hackathons?

No, we want the hackathons to be for everyone. So we do not charge anything from the participants or the organizers.

If it is a Cycode affiliated event, then who would be incharge of the decision making process?

For the organizational part on ground, you , as the organizer, is the incharge of the decision making process. We will still be available for your help. The sponsorships and other organizational decisions are mutual, between the organizer and Cycode.

For Hackers:

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event organized in which students and professionals from all backgrounds come together under one stage to collaborate on software and hardware projects. They might last between 24 to 72 hours. They are generally based on a specific theme or focus.

Who can participate?

Basically, EVERYONE. All students, professionals from all backgrounds are welcome. If you don't know coding, don't worry we are here to help you.

What if I am a newbie?

Don’t worry if you are a beginner, Hackathon is all about learning. We’ll be conducting a seminars prior to hackathons to fill you up with ideas and volunteers will be there throughout the event to help you out, make your team and you are all for CYCODE hackathons.

Will I get travel reimbursement?

No, we are not providing travel reimbursement for eligible hackers. If we get the appropraiate funds from our sponsors, then we all the applicants will be notified.

What should I bring?

Get your laptop and cellphone and their chargers. In addition, bringing a water bottle, pillow, and any other accessories you would bring for a sleepover would be a good idea.

Who all can participate and what are the incentives?

Any under-graduate or post-graduate student, who’s passionate, is welcome. Besides the winning team’s prize every participant will receive a certificate and indeed, a whole new experience.
